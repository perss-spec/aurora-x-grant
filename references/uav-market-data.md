## Key Facts for Proposal
- >50,000 eVTOL expected by 2030, each requiring 4-36 motors
- 98% of EU rare earth magnets imported from China
- Gap between lab (>10 kW/kg) and certified (5 kW/kg) motor performance
- Clean Aviation Call 4: EUR 329.5M for aviation decarbonization (2026)
- Power electronics target: from 20 kW/kg today to 80 kW/kg by 2040
